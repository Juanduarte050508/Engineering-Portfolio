# 🏎️ F1 in Schools - Alset Brasil
**Role: Assistant Engineer**

### 🏁 Project Description
F1 in Schools is a global competition where students design and manufacture miniature F1 cars. As part of **Alset Brasil**, I contributed to the engineering department.

### 🔧 My Responsibilities
- **Aerodynamic Optimization:** Assisting in car body adjustments for better performance.
- **Technical Regulations:** Ensuring the car met all official competition safety and measurement standards.
- **Manufacturing Support:** Helping bridge the gap between CAD design and the final CNC-machined model.

### 🏆 Team Experience
Working with Alset Brasil provided deep insights into professional engineering workflows, team collaboration, and high-precision manufacturing.
