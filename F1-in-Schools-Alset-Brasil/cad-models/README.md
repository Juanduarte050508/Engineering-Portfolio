## 📐 3D Modeling & CAD Development
All components were designed using **Autodesk Fusion 360**, following a modular approach to optimize weight distribution and assembly speed.

### Highlights:
*   **Aerofoil Profile:** Developed the rear wing using the **NACA 4412** profile, optimized for a -8° Angle of Attack (AoA) to balance downforce and drag.
*   **Iterative Design:** The wheel and chassis versions (up to v20) demonstrate a rigorous optimization process based on CFD feedback.
*   **Standard Compliance:** Integrated official FIA-style components such as the `halo_v2` and `bell_helmet` for technical regulation compliance.
